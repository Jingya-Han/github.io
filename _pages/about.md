Hi! I am **Jingya**. I am now a PhD candidate in Atmospheric Sciences at Cornell University. My main area of expertise is the characteristics of precipitation.

My research currently mainly focuses on precipitation intensity distributions, climate model evaluation, and the mechanisms driving hydroclimate change.

## Research Interests

- Precipitation
- Climate model evaluation
- Aerosol forcing and hydroclimate response

## Education

- PhD in Atmospheric Science, Cornell University
- B.S. in Atmospheric Science, Northeast Normal University
